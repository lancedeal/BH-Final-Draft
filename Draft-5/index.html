Our Mission

Bad Hog Land Clearing is a small business locally owned and operated out of San Marcos, Texas. Our team is a tight-knit group of dedicated individuals who share a passion for land clearing. We take pride in our work, and are committed to bringing you quality results as quickly and safely as possible.

Land Clearing

Land clearing is the first step for any construction project. Whether that's having fencing installed, or laying foundation for a house, you need the space to do it! That's where we come in; we'll lay the groundwork for your project by removing any vegetation standing in the way.

Tree Removal

Once we've discussed the scope and parameters of the project with you, we'll use a combination of power tools and one or more Skid Steers to cutdown and consolidate the brush on your property.

Disposal Methods

After the brush has been consolidated, we move on to disposal. Depending on your preference, we can burn or mulch the brush. At this time, we do not offer haul-off.

When such large piles of brush are involved, burning can be dangerous. We'll utilize the Skid Steer to manage the size of the fire, and only burn when drought conditions permit us to safely do so.

As for mulching, we'll proceed with a Chipper Shredder or Tub Grinder, and work in conjunction with the Skid Steer and ground personnel to process the material.
